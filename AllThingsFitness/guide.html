  • For Beginners: Introduction to basic exercises, 3-day full-body workout plans, bodyweight routines.

  • Form and Technique: Detailed instructions on performing exercises safely.
  • Common Mistakes: Identifying and correcting errors in exercise form.
  • Injury Prevention: Prehab exercises, Warm-up routines, Proper equipment use.
  • Recovery Strategies: Cool-down exercises, Stretching post-workout, Use of recovery tools.

2.2 Micronutrients

  • Vitamins: Importance of vitamins A, B-complex, C, D, E, and K.
  • Minerals: Essential minerals like calcium, magnesium, potassium, and zinc.
  • Electrolytes: Sodium, potassium, magnesium balance in hydration and performance.

2.3 Diet Types and Plans

  • Standard Diets: Balanced diet, Low-fat diet, High-protein diet.

  • Overcoming Plateaus: Identifying causes, Strategies to break through plateaus.
  • Reward Systems: Setting up a reward system for achieving milestones.

6.3 Youth Fitness

  • Sports Participation: Benefits, Safety considerations, Avoiding overtraining.

6.4 Fitness with Chronic Conditions

  • Heart Disease: Safe cardiovascular exercises, Importance of gradual progression.
  • Arthritis: Low-impact exercises, Flexibility and mobility focus, Managing pain.
